3.The Classic Ambassador

HINDUSTAN AMBASSADOR
The Hindustan Ambassador is an automobile that was manufactured by Hindustan Motors of India from 1957 to 2014, with improvements and changes over its production lifetime. The Ambassador was based on the Morris Oxford series III model, first made by Morris Motors Limited at Cowley, Oxford in the United Kingdom from 1956 to 1959.Despite its British origins, the Ambassador was considered as a definitive Indian car and was fondly called the "King of Indian roads"
